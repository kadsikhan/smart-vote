About SmartVote

SmartVote is a modern, secure voting platform designed for your FYP project.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a simple yet powerful voting system that can be used for various purposes, from student elections to project feedback collection.

Features

  • Create custom polls with multiple options
  • Secure voting process
  • Real-time results with visual charts
  • Easy poll management with delete functionality
  • Responsive design that works on all devices

How It Works

  1. Create a poll with your question and options
  2. Share the poll with participants
  3. Participants vote on their preferred options
  4. View real-time results with visual charts
  5. Delete polls when they're no longer needed
This platform was developed as part of a Final Year Project (FYP) to demonstrate modern web development techniques.
